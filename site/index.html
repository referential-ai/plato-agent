Why Platonic

A common foundation, made concrete.

Platonic names the framework and the standard it pursues: precise boundaries, inspectable work, and replayable outcomes. Plato Agent is the reference application that makes those commitments tangible.

Diligence by design

Visible by construction.

The ledger is the run. Interfaces, transcripts, recovery, and audits are derived from recorded facts rather than hidden control flow.
